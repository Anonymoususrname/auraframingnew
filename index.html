Our Story

We Are Aura Framing

Since 2010

Born from a love of love.

Aura Framing was founded with a single belief — every couple deserves a wedding captured in a way that perfectly reflects their unique love story. What began as a boutique photography studio has grown into a full-service luxury wedding company trusted across India and beyond.

Our team brings together expertise in photography, videography, floral design, event planning, and hospitality — united by one purpose: to make your most important day extraordinary in every way.

We don't just capture weddings. We frame memories, preserve emotions, and create a timeless visual story of the most beautiful chapter of your life.

Common Questions

Package FAQs

What's the difference between Traditional and Candid photography? +
Traditional photography covers key posed moments — rituals, family portraits, and formal shots. Candid captures natural, unposed emotions and behind-the-scenes moments throughout the day.
When will I receive my album and video? +
Albums and edited videos are typically delivered within 4–6 weeks after the wedding. We keep you updated throughout the entire editing process.
Can the outdoor shoot be done before the wedding? +
Yes — the complimentary outdoor shoot in Package 02 can be done as a pre-wedding or post-wedding session, entirely at your preference and convenience.
Can I customise a package? +
Absolutely. All packages can be adjusted to add or remove items. Contact us and we'll create a tailored quote to match exactly what you need.
How do I confirm my booking? +
Simply WhatsApp us at +91 77086 97816 or fill the booking form. We'll confirm your date and send you the next steps right away.
